Food culture in Bangalore!

For South Indian lovers, definitely check out these places

  1. MTR

    If you love dosas as much as I do and wake up on Sundays with insatiable cravings of Rava dosa then this place might just be for you

  2. CTR

    Comfort South Indian food has never been better served and tasted than this classic heritage hotel in Malleshwaram

  3. Adigas

    All Bengalurians keep this place at the back of their heads when it comes to some quick breakfast that you can grab on the go

If you love Continental/Western Cuisine you'll definitely love these places

  1. Hard Rock Cafe

    The multi-national franchise has laid one of it's signature pubs in Bangalore if you're in it for some great food and drinks. From Burgers to pastas all your cravings will be satisfied

  2. Truffles

    A budget friendly delicious restaurant that has your Indian, Chinese and Italian cravings? No worries, Truffles has got your back at any corner of the city with is multiple outlets

  3. Smallys

    It's small, cute but big on the portions, check it out for yourself

If you've got a sweet tooth you, these are places you can't miss

  1. Corner House

    If you haven't had Bangalore's favorite dessert, The Death By Chocolate; my friend you are definitely missing out.

  2. KC Das

    Traditional Indian mithai that will take your taste buds to another planet. The rasgullas are to die for.

  3. Milano Ice-Cream

    Hands down the best gelato in Bangalore
